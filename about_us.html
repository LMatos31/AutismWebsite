Autism

My name is Leandro Matos, born 31st January 2000 in Pretoria, South Africa

The reason I have created this website is to bring more awareness to Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) as not many people actually know what autism is and the difficulties that people who are diagnosed with autism suffer with. I have noticed that people who do not know what autism is struggle to understand why people who have it act differently to other. It is difficult to intereact with them as they have not developeted the skill to be in a social enviroment. I want people to understand how to deal with those who have ASD and I also want parents whos child might be acting in a specific way know what the symptoms of ASD are so they can get help from specialists or therapists who deal with this disorder.
