Autism

Autism better known as ASD (Autism Spectrum Disorder) is a lifelong developmental disability that typically appears during the early stages of childhood and can impact a person’s social skills, communication, relationships, and self-regulation. Autism is defined as a certain set of behaviors and is a spectrum condition that affects people differently and to a deifferent extent.

What is Autism?

Symptom that your child has Autism

  • They speak later than typical or not at all
  • They do not like change for example, change in their schedule or change in their food
  • They only have interest in specific things such as a part of a toy
  • They do not use typical form of non verbal communication like avoiding eye contact with a person or not showing any facial expressions
  • They prefer to be alone adn do not want to socialize with people
  • They tend to repeat certain words and they also repeat certain movements
  • They are very sensitive to loud noises
  • They also have issues with sleeping

How to diagnose someone with Autism

As of now there is still no test to diagnose is a child has autism, so doctors tend to use the childs behavioural history to determine whether they have ASD. The doctors start by questioning the parent about the child at the early stages of their live. Some of the question they ask are:

  • Did your child tend to repeat a certain sound or word when they were 9 months old?
  • Did your child smile at the age of 6 months?
  • Do you notice any unusual behaviour?
  • Does he/she respond when someone is trying to interact with them?
  • Does he/she get angry very easily?
  • Is he/she sensitive to loud noises?
  • Does your child interact with other kids in a social enviroment?

The Doctors use the answers from the parent to conclude whether the child has ASD or not so it is crucial that the parent answer the question properly to ensure the best result for the doctors to conduct the test.
